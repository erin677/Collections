In Progress Pc

My PC build began in January 2021 when I first became interested in computers when my ex boyfriend introduced them to me. It began with the case and spread from there. Of course there were a few bumps in the road, especially with the lack of graphics cards on the market.
At the moment, the PC works but only for a few moments and then it overheats, I have to reapply the thermal paste on the CPU, which I have been ignoring for months because getting the CPU on in the first place was the equivalent of running a marathon. And so it sits on my desk as the most expensive paper weight I have ever owned in my life.
